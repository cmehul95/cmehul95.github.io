No 1 About Project

My primary project as an intern was to lead the research on pool rides to make a more focused product for the Indian market. I used the Diary study method to understand the bird eye perspective of the problem space, further also probed into certain essential themes representative of Pool

No 2 The Brief

Researching on the behaviour of high frequency riders, understand the major pain points of the users and the typical customer journeys

No 3 The Process

Since the aim was to understand the behaviour of high frequency pool riders, we opted for Diary Study to essentially get their daily experience logs for an extended period. We made the method a bit organic in the sense that we intervened every third day to probe 3 primary themes that were specific to high frequency pool riders. For daily logs, Google Forms were used as the participants were not tech savvy. Further, I used to follow up over whatsapp whenever I needed to gain a deeper understanding

No 4 Participants

The Diary Study consisted a fairly homogenous group of participants spread over all the major cities in India like NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Ahmedabad and Jaipur. All of them were commuters, using Uber primarily for office and college commute

No 5 Insights

The plethora of data that I collected from the 10 day long study was transcribed into post-it notes. Affinity analysis of the notes revealed four major themes. Further, the qualitative analysis also helped me to formulated the flywheels of Commute and the essential Customer Journeys. Then, I deep dived into each of the themes to discover four primary pain points
