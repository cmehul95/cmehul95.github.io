Towards Immersive Conversations

See the detail

No 1 About Project

I am doing this Thesis Project under the guidance of Prof. Ravi Mokashi Punekar as a mandatory part of my Bachelor Studies. The aim of the project is to infuse the element of reality in the current chat conversations making them more close to the face to face conversations. The project is still ongoing.

No 2 The Brief

Studying the way people communicate through different modes, design a solution to make the current chat conversations more immersive and close to the real face to face conversations.

No 3 The Process

I decided to follow the double diamond design process to help me in the design thinking process and to come up with a viable solution. I started off with an initial Literature Research to find the gaps in research and also to understand the domain space. After that, User Research and Direct Observation was conducted to get qualitative insights. Finally, I proceeded to develop certain concepts on the basis of the research.

No 4 Literature Review

I read a number of research papers related to Computer Mediated Communication(CMC), Face to Face Communication(FtF), Emoticon Usage and Relational Maintenance. The literature review helped me to better understand the domain space.

No 5 User Research

First, I studied the chats of six subjects, each subject was asked to produce the exerpts for friends and family. The chats were analysed and insights were drawn. To further dive deep into the real face to face conversations and get a first hand experience at the sort of cues involved, a Direct Research was conducted. The study involved an analysis of the recordings of groups of people conversing face to face. The results of both the studies are given below.

No 6 Analysis

The cues discovered in the Direct Observation were analysed and classified into two senses: Sight and Hearing. Later, an elemental mapping of the cues was done to understand how those cues could be reformed in software.

No 7 Exploration

Developing on the insights from the Research, I proceeded to explore certain concepts which would further help me in coming up with the final solution.

No 8 Defining Concept

Considering the technological constraints in the other ideas, I decided to proceed with the text animations to depict emotions
