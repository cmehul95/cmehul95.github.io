No 1 About Project

As an intern, I worked on amazonbusiness.in which is the retail wholesale vertical of amazon. This product allows the local resellers to buy goods from Amazon in wholesale. My task was to improve the discovery and navigation experiences of the gateway. I was single handedly involved from the User Research till the final deliverable of a working prototype.

No 2 The Brief

Research and design best in breed discoverability and navigation experiences centered around the gateway of amazonbusiness.in

No 3 The Process

Understanding the Design Brief, I proceeded to conduct Research. This helped me to discover the customer Pain Points. I later set five Objectives to address the pain points. Then, I generated Design Blurbs to meet each objective. This process was crucial for me to fulfill the initial Design Brief.

No 4 Current Scenario

Right now, the mobile gateway consists of mainly three parts for discovery- Search, Promotions and Brand Farm

No 5 Data Study

I used the feature analyser tool of Amazon to study the data metrics for 1 month. Later, the data was studied to form valuable insights. The original data cannot be revealed to comply with NDA.

No 6 Shadowing

To get an initial understanding of the end users and their environment, I accompanied one FOS(Feet on Street) employee to meet two customers. I also asked the retailers to try their hands on the amazonbusiness.in website.

No 7 Customer Interviews

Next, I went on the field and interviewed four existing customers of amazonbusiness.in. The interviews were recorded and later analysed to gather insights.

No 8 Analysis

Analysing the insights from Data Metrics, Customers Visits and a Contextual Inquiry Report of the User Research team, I chalked down the Customer Painpoints. Later, I derived five objectives from the customer painpoints.
