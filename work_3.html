Swadesi, Semester Thesis Project

See the detail

No 1 About Project

The duration of the project was one month. I worked in a group of three people which included two of my batchmates, Dhruv and Isha. We chose to work on the Indian Handicraft Industry which has been struggling in the recent times. After a comprihensive study of the whole system and considering the possible intervention points we came up with the concept 'Swadesi'.

No 2 The Brief

Considering the stunted growth of the Indian Handicrafts Industry, study the overall system and propose a solution to uplift the Industry.

No 3 The Process

Initially, we started off with Literature Research which included the study of a number of research papers and government websites. Gathering the relevant information, a SWOT Analysis of the current scenario was conducted. Further, a comprehensive study of the whole System was done to identify the crucial Systems, Subsystems and their Elements. Then, a short User Study was done to identify and define the end consumers which helped us in forming two User Archetypes. Proceeding, the concept 'Swadesi' was developed along with Data Flow Diagram of the whole model.

No 4 SWOT Analysis

After reading a number of research papers, articles and govt. websites a SWOT Analysis of the existing scenario was conducted to locate the crucial elements.

No 5 System Analysis

The Handicraft Industry was broken down into the basic Subsystems and their Elements using the Systems Approach to Design. Further, the gaps between the Elements of various Subsystems were detailed out.

No 6 User Study

The process started with defining key objectives and then framing a detailed questionarie. Further, user studies were conducted which helped to understand the current scenario and the corresponding user behavior. Analysis of the user interviews gave us two major archetypes, “The Everyday Buyer” and “Travellers & Tourists”

No 7 Concept

Keeping in mind the possible User Base, the concept 'Swadesi' was developed. 'Swadesi' is an online marketplace for the sale of Indian Handicrafts. The whole Data Flow Diagram of the concept was also developed to check the viability of the concept.
