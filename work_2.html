No 1 About Project

The duration of the project was 10 days. I chose to redefine Public Libraries as they support personal productivity and cultural engagement of the community. After an initial Literature Research and Analysis, I developed the concept 'LIT'. The concept is a mobile application which works in collaboration with Philips Hue Lights to improve the experience of Public Libraries.

No 2 The Brief

Proposals should suit the context of a public space interior or exterior and should answer a social need while improving quality of life and having a positive impact on the community, all through the personalization of lighting.

No 3 The Process

After choosing the space as Public Library I proceeded to conduct Literature Research, on the basis of which I created two Personas. Then, a Scenario was developed for each persona which were later Analysed to discover possible Opportunity Areas. Finally, I proceeded to the Concept Generation phase to develop my concept LIT.

No 4 Literature Research

I read various research papers, news articles and Public library reports to gather the relevant information.

No 5 Personas

After the Literature Research, I proceeded to create the Primary(Sarah) and Secondary(Jack) personas. The Personas are inspired from the information provided about the users in major library reports.

No 6 Scenario

Then, a Scenario was chalked out for each persona to figure out the pain points of the users.

No 7 Analysis

Analysing the scenario of each persona, I listed down some areas which could be enhanced by the Personalisation of Lighting

No 8 Concept

Exploring the Opportunity Areas, I came up with the Concept 'LIT'. LIT is software solution to personalize the lighting in a public library. LIT works in collaboration with Philips Hue lights to enhance the Library Experience.
